Deep Dish Brownies

Prep time

60 mins

Servings

9

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter,melted
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup all=purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)and grease an 8 inch square span, In a large bowl,blend melted butter,sugar and vanilla.
  2. Beat in eggs one at a time.
  3. Combine the flour,cocoa,baking powder and salt.Gradually blend into the egg mixture .
  4. Spread the butter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake in pre heated oven for 40 to 45 minutes,or until brownies begin to pull away from the two sides of the pan.
  6. Let brownies cool,then cut into squares,Enjoy!

Tip

Aluminium foil can be used to keep food moist,cook it evenly,and make clean-up easier.
